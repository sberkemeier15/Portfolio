My Journey

I spent years working as an ultrasound tech, where paying close attention to detail was part of the daily grind. Along the way, I found myself drawn to design creating things that are not just useful but also feel right. Switching gears into UX and web design has been an exciting adventure, mixing my love for problem-solving with creativity. I’m all about making digital experiences that connect with people and tell a story, one step at a time.

My Projects

Mindful Leaf

A calming, nature-inspired web design focused on mindfulness and stress relief. Crafted with soothing earth tones and intuitive, minimalistic layouts to create a peaceful user experience that invites daily reflection and balance.
